About the School..........

HSS THIRUVAMPADY is state govt affiliated higher secondary school situated near to the Pazhaveed devi temple Alappuzha Tht school has all the lab facilities that children can explore and learn.Its was established in 1939 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in ALAPPUZHA block of ALAPPUZHA district of Kerala. The school has Private building. It has got 11 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Well and it is functional. The school has 25 boys toilet and it is functional. and 15 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 5000 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 30 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab. The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal. The school provide a wide range of facilities for children to explore.The school has a full fledged smart class sytsem in each classes and a computer lab containing about 30 systems and 12 laptops.
